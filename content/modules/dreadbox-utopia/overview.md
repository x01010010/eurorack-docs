# Overview

**UTOPIA** is a 10 HP CV and audio manipulation utility from Dreadbox. It brings together five distinct functions in a single compact panel: four attenuverters, an offset generator, a 4-to-3 mixer with buffered splitter operation, a voltage-controlled LFO, and the Pulserizer distortion effect.

Part of Dreadbox's Chromatic Modules series, **UTOPIA** is designed to glue a patch together — scaling and inverting CVs, adding DC offsets, mixing audio or modulation, generating slow-to-audio-rate modulation, and mangling signals into aggressive square waves. Every section is independently usable, and several can interact with each other for more complex functions.

## At a Glance

- **10 HP**, 23 mm deep
- **4× Attenuverters** — scale and invert CV or audio signals
- **Offset Generator** — adds or subtracts up to ±10 V
- **4-to-3 Mixer** — sums four inputs to three outputs; also works as a buffered splitter
- **Voltage-Controlled LFO** — 30 s to 440 Hz, swept by CV
- **Pulserizer** — distortion effect that converts signals to square waves
- **Power**: 40 mA (+12 V) / 27 mA (−12 V)
