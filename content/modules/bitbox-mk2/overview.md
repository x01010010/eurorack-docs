# Overview

**BITBOX MK2** is a 26 HP Eurorack sampler from 1010music, built around a 3.5-inch touchscreen that makes sample selection, editing, and clip management fast enough to use in a live performance context. It records and plays back samples, launches clips from a 16-slot grid, slices loops, applies granular processing, and streams files directly from microSD — including samples up to 4 GB that would be too large to hold in RAM.

The module ships with bitbox firmware but the hardware also accepts two alternative firmwares from 1010music: **fxbox** for stereo effects processing (reverb, delay, chorus, and others), and **synthbox** for wavetable synthesis. All three are free downloads, and switching between them only requires loading the firmware file onto the microSD card.

CV and MIDI connectivity make **BITBOX MK2** deeply integrable with the rest of a modular system. Eight CV inputs can be mapped to virtually any parameter on screen. MIDI in and out via TRS jacks allows communication with hardware sequencers, DAWs, and other MIDI-equipped modules. Internal mixing, per-clip effects, and configurable preset saving round out a module that can serve as the centrepiece of a self-contained performance rig.

| Depth | ~32 mm (behind panel) |
| Power | 400 mA +12 V / 0 mA −12 V |
| Audio resolution | 24-bit ADC and DAC |
| Sample formats | WAV, 16/24/32-bit, any sample rate (48 kHz preferred) |
| Storage | microSD (streaming up to 4 GB per file) |

| CV inputs | 8 |
