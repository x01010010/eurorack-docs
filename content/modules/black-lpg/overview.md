# Overview

**BLACK LPG** is a 10 HP vactrol-based low-pass gate from Erica Synths, designed from the ground up as a modern take on the classic Buchla-style LPG topology. Where the original Buchla 292 used a single vactrol for a first-order response, Black LPG uses three vactrols to achieve a second-order (12 dB/oct) low-pass filter, paired with a VCA in a single signal path. A resonance circuit with switchable overdrive adds harmonic aggression not found in traditional LPGs.

The built-in decay envelope generator means you can create plucked, organic tones with just a trigger — no external envelope needed. The decay time is both manually and voltage controllable, and the envelope has its own output jack so it can modulate other modules in your patch.

## Sonic Territory

- **Woody plucks and bongos** — the classic LPG sound. Patch a trigger, set a short decay, and the vactrol response produces naturally percussive attacks with smooth release.
- **Resonant filter sweeps** — in VCF mode with resonance cranked, it behaves like a characterful 2-pole low-pass filter.
- **Overdrive growl** — flip the overdrive switch and the resonance circuit saturates into screaming, aggressive territory.
- **Transparent VCA** — in VCA-only mode, it's a clean amplitude controller with vactrol smoothing.
- **Combined dynamics** — VCF+VCA mode simultaneously shapes both timbre and volume, mimicking acoustic instrument behavior where quieter = darker.

## At a Glance

- **10 HP** Eurorack module, 35 mm deep
- **3 vactrols** for second-order (12 dB/oct) low-pass response
- **3 modes** — VCF, VCF+VCA, VCA (manually and CV switchable)
- **Built-in decay envelope** with manual and CV control (up to 4 seconds)
- **Envelope CV output** for modulating other modules
- **Resonance with overdrive switch**
- **Power**: 44 mA (+12 V) / 45 mA (-12 V)
