# Overview

**BLACK OCTASOURCE** is a 12 HP syncable LFO from Erica Synths with eight simultaneous outputs. Its two operating modes make it fundamentally different tools: in Single mode it acts as a phase-shifted quad/octa LFO (one waveform, eight phases 45° apart); in Multi mode it outputs all eight waveforms at once, and the WAVE knob cycles them across the outputs.

Rate, waveform selection, and phase shift are all CV controllable. The LFO syncs to an external clock via the SYNC input. Setting the RATE knob to 12 o'clock freezes the LFO at its current state — useful for sample-and-hold-like snapshots. Output polarity can be switched between bipolar (±5 V) and unipolar (0–+5 V) via a hidden calibration gesture.

## Sonic Territory

- **Quadrature / phase-offset modulation** — eight outputs at 45° increments create sweeping, spatial chorus and phasing effects when routed to multiple oscillator pitches or filter cutoffs.
- **8-waveform modulation bus** — Multi mode delivers every waveform shape simultaneously, letting you pick the right curve for each destination without separate LFOs.
- **Tempo-synced modulation** — SYNC locks the LFO to an external clock for rhythmically precise sweeps.
- **Generative panning** — feed adjacent outputs to panner CV inputs for slow, rotating stereo movement.
- **Complex LFO shapes** — patch one output back into WAVE CV for self-modulating, evolving waveform selection.

## At a Glance

- **12 HP**, 25 mm deep
- **8 simultaneous outputs** (±5 V bipolar default; switchable to 0–+5 V unipolar)
- **Single mode**: one waveform, phase-shifted 45° per output
- **Multi mode**: all 8 waveforms across outputs; WAVE knob rotates assignments
- **8 waveforms** with smooth morphing between them
- **CV control** over rate (FM), waveform, and phase
- **External clock sync** (SYNC input)
- **LFO freeze** at RATE = 12 o'clock
- **Power**: 82 mA (+12 V) / 44 mA (−12 V)
