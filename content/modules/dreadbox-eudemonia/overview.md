# Overview

**EUDEMONIA** is a 10 HP analog signal processor from Dreadbox combining three functions in one panel: a 3-to-1 mixer, a dual filter, and an exponential VCA. It is designed as an all-in-one voice channel — blend multiple sources, filter them, and control amplitude — without needing separate modules for each stage.

The filter section runs both filters in series: a 24 dB/oct resonant low-pass followed by an 18 dB/oct non-resonant high-pass. Running in series rather than parallel means the low-pass and high-pass work as a band-pass pair, with the passband determined by the relationship between their cutoff frequencies. The LP has resonance capable of self-oscillation; the HP shapes the low end without adding its own resonance.

The exponential VCA has a built-in peak LED that lights above 10 VPTP, helping monitor levels before clipping downstream. LED indicators on every section show the real-time CV sum, making modulation routing visible at a glance.

**EUDEMONIA** is part of the Dreadbox Chromatic Modules series. A blacklight-reactive alternative panel is available (compatible with v1.1 only).

## At a Glance

- **10 HP**, 35 mm deep
- **3-to-1 mixer** — blend up to three sources
- **Dual filter in series**: 24 dB resonant LP + 18 dB non-resonant HP
- **Exponential VCA** with peak indicator (above 10 VPTP)
- **LED indicators** per section showing CV sum
- **Power**: 61 mA (+12 V) / 69 mA (−12 V)
- Series: **Dreadbox Chromatic Modules**
