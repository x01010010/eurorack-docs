# Panel

The Neutron panel is divided into functional sections left to right.

## Oscillators (VCO 1 & VCO 2)

- **RANGE** — octave selector per oscillator
- **TUNE** — fine pitch per oscillator
- **WAVE** — waveform blend (tone mod → saw → pulse → triangle → sine)
- **PW / TONE MOD** — pulse width / tone mod depth per oscillator
- **VCO MIX** — blend between VCO 1 and VCO 2

## Noise / External Input

- **NOISE** level knob
- **EXT IN** — external audio input with level control

## VCF

- **CUTOFF** — filter frequency
- **RESONANCE** — self-oscillating at maximum
- **ENV AMT** — filter envelope modulation depth
- **DRIVE** — overdrive amount into the filter
- **MODE** switch — LP / HP / LP+HP
- LP OUT and HP OUT jacks

## Envelopes (ENV 1 and ENV 2)

- Independent ATTACK, DECAY, SUSTAIN, RELEASE per envelope
- ENV 1 normalled to VCF; ENV 2 normalled to VCA

## VCA

- **LEVEL** — manual VCA level (opens VCA without gate)

## LFO

- **RATE** — speed (with MIDI clock sync option)
- **WAVE** — five shapes: sine, triangle, sawtooth, reverse saw, square
- **KEY SYNC** and **DELAY/FADE IN** switches

## Delay (BBD)

- **TIME** — delay time
- **FEEDBACK** — regeneration
- **MIX** — dry/wet blend

## Sample & Hold

- **GLIDE** — slew applied to S&H output
- **CLOCK IN** / **SIGNAL IN** / **OUT** jacks

## Utilities

- **ATTENUATORS** (×2) — unipolar attenuators with jacks
- **MULTIPLES** — passive mult jacks
- **SLEW** — slew rate limiter with IN/OUT
- **SUMMERS** (×2) — two-input summers with output jack

## MIDI / USB

- MIDI IN (5-pin DIN)
- USB Type-B connector
- **MIDI CH** selector
