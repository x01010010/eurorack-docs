## CV / Trigger I/O

All trigger and CV jacks are 3.5 mm TS, DC coupled.

| Jack | Direction | Range / Level |
| --- | --- | --- |
| TRIG OUT 1–10 | Out | +5 V trigger |
| ACCENT OUT 1–10 | Out | +5 V trigger |
| CV OUT | Out | ±10 V |
| GATE OUT | Out | +5 V gate |
| CLK OUT | Out | +5 V clock |
| CLK IN | In | +5 V tolerant |
| RESET / GATE IN | In | +5 V tolerant |

Patch points: 22 outputs, 2 inputs.
