# Overview

The 121 is a dual 24 dB/octave voltage-controlled low-pass filter based on the Roland System 100M 121 Dual VCF. Each of the two independent filter channels provides three signal inputs with individual level controls, three modulation inputs, and a switchable high-pass filter mode.

The 24 dB/oct slope produces the classic Roland ladder filter character — smooth, warm low-pass with resonance that reaches self-oscillation. Having two independent filters in one module makes the 121 especially useful for parallel filter paths, stereo filtering, and crossover-style frequency splitting.
