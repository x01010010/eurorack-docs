# Overview

**HYSTERIA** is a 10 HP full analog voltage-controlled oscillator from Dreadbox, part of their Chromatic series. It pairs a wide-range VCO core (16 Hz – 32 kHz) with a voltage-controlled waveshaper and a built-in VC quantizer, making it equally suited for precise melodic work and wild timbral exploration.

Unlike digital oscillators that rely on wavetable lookup, Hysteria generates all its waveforms in the analog domain and then sculpts them through a continuous waveshaper circuit. The quantizer locks incoming pitch CV to semitones, letting you drive it from unquantized sources (LFOs, random, touch controllers) and still land on musical notes.

## Sonic Territory

- **Classic analog leads** — pure triangle through hard-edged square, with everything in between via the waveshaper.
- **Sync screams** — hard sync input produces aggressive, harmonically rich timbres when swept.
- **Clocked pulse trains** — the dedicated pulse output doubles as a clock source for sequencers and other modules.
- **Detuned stacks** — pair two Hysterias (or sync one to another VCO) for thick unison patches.
- **Sub-audio modulation** — the 16 Hz floor means it can function as an LFO in a pinch.

## At a Glance

- **10 HP** Eurorack module, 25 mm deep
- **Full analog** VCO core, 16 Hz – 32 kHz
- **7 waveforms** via continuous waveshaper
- **VC Quantizer** — locks pitch to semitones on/off via button
- **Hard Sync** input for timbral sync effects
- **Dedicated Pulse output** for clocking or syncing other oscillators
- **Power**: 58 mA (+12 V) / 15 mA (-12 V)
