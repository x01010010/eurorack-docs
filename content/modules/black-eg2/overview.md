# Overview

**BLACK EG2** is a single-channel exponential ADSR envelope generator from Erica Synths in 8 HP. It provides a full Attack–Decay–Sustain–Release envelope with retriggering, an internal gate generator with adjustable gate length, and a looping mode that turns the envelope into a complex LFO.

The standout feature is the gate length circuit: most envelope generators require a held gate signal to reach the sustain stage, meaning short triggers only produce AD shapes. Black EG2 solves this with an internal gate generator that extends any incoming trigger to an adjustable gate length — so you get full ADSR envelopes even from trigger-only sequencers. The gate length is both manually and CV controllable.

In loop mode, the envelope cycles continuously through all four ADSR stages (not just AD like most looping envelopes), producing complex, customizable waveshapes suitable for modulation duties.

## Sonic Territory

- **Snappy percussive envelopes** — short attack, zero sustain, short decay for plucks and clicks.
- **Slow swells** — long attack times for ambient pads and gradual filter opens.
- **Complex LFO shapes** — loop mode produces asymmetric, multi-stage waveforms that simple LFOs can't match.
- **Trigger-driven ADSR** — full envelope contours from clock/trigger sequencers without needing a gate-length module.
- **Rhythmic modulation** — CV over gate length creates per-step sustain variation from a uniform trigger stream.

## At a Glance

- **8 HP** Eurorack module, 25 mm deep
- **Single** ADSR envelope channel
- **Exponential** envelope curves
- **Re-triggerable** — new trigger restarts the envelope immediately
- **Internal gate generator** with manual and CV-controlled gate length
- **Loop mode** per channel — full ADSR cycling
- **Manual gate buttons** for auditioning without patching
- **Power**: 29 mA (+12 V) / 33 mA (-12 V)
