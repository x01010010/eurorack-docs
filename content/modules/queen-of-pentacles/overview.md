# Overview

**QUEEN OF PENTACLES** is a 30 HP seven-voice hybrid drum synthesizer from Endorphin.es, drawing its analog voice architecture from the Roland TR-909. Three discrete analog circuits handle bass drum, snare, and clap — each using band-limited binary noise generators with adjustable noise spectrum animation (the "Spoiler" knob). Four sample-based channels play audio directly from a microSD card at zero latency, with factory content including processed Paiste 602 cymbals (hi-hats, ride, crash) prepared by Nicolas Bougaïeff.

The onboard "Cabin Pressure" effect processor offers 16 effects across two banks (Airways and Darkwaves), from hall and shimmer reverbs to ring modulation and a freezer/looper. A master isolator-style LP/HP filter with VCA control and saturator booster shapes the final mix. Every channel has its own trigger input, accent/velocity CV input, level control, and individual output jack. Rolls are clocked per channel.

## Sonic Territory

- **TR-909-derived analog kick and snare** with drive, tune, and decay sculpting.
- **Custom sample kits** — swap the four sample channels with your own sounds via SD card.
- **Effect-soaked fills** — route the entire kit or individual voices through reverbs, delays, tape echo, or the ring modulator.
- **Live performance** — mute switches, manual trigger buttons, accent inputs, and clockable rolls make it stage-ready without a sequencer.
- **Parallel processing** — individual outputs let you route each voice to separate FX chains or mixer channels.

## At a Glance

- **30 HP**, 26 mm deep
- **3 analog voices**: bass drum, snare drum, hand clap
- **4 sample voices** (S1–S4): zero-latency playback from microSD
- **8 sample banks** (factory + custom), selectable per session
- **16-effect processor** (2 banks of 8): reverbs, delays, looper, ring mod, and more
- **Master LP/HP isolator filter** with saturator booster
- **Individual outputs** per voice (stereo pair for S1/S2, S3/S4)
- **Trigger + accent/velocity input** per channel
- **Clockable rolls** on every channel
- **Power**: 350 mA (+12 V) / 90 mA (-12 V)
