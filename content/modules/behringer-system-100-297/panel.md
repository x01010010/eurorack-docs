# Panel

The 297 panel divides into three clear sections from top to bottom.

## Portamento A and B

Two identical portamento channels occupy the upper two-thirds of the panel. Each has:

- **RISE** — slew rate for rising CV transitions (knob)
- **FALL** — slew rate for falling CV transitions (knob)
- **IN** — CV input jack
- **OUT** — slew-processed CV output jack

RISE and FALL operate independently, allowing asymmetric glide curves. At minimum, glide is effectively off; at maximum, transitions take several seconds.

## CV Mixer 1 and 2

Two identical two-input mixer sections occupy the lower third:

- **IN 1** with attenuator knob
- **IN 2** with attenuator knob
- **OUT** — summed output jack

Both mixers are passive-style unity or attenuating — they sum and scale but do not amplify above the input level.
