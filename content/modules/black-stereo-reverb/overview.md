# Overview

**BLACK STEREO REVERB** is a 12 HP high-fidelity stereo reverb module from Erica Synths, part of the Black Series. It runs on Erica Synths' custom DSP hardware with reverb algorithms developed in collaboration with Dutch software company 112dB, known for their audiophile-quality audio tools.

The module offers three reverb source types — Tape, BBD, and dirty BBD delay line emulations — each available in three room modes: Room, Hall, and Cathedral. This gives nine distinct reverb characters ranging from intimate and warm to vast and ethereal. Size, feedback, feedback tone, and stereo spin are all independently controllable and CV-addressable.

A dedicated FREEZE function holds the reverb tail indefinitely — triggerable manually or via CV gate — making it useful for live performance swells and infinite pad generation. Ten preset slots allow saving and recalling complete reverb configurations, with the active preset also selectable via CV.

**BLACK STEREO REVERB** accepts stereo input, produces stereo output at 48 kHz / 24-bit, and provides a dry/wet mix with CV control.

## At a Glance

- **12 HP**, 35 mm deep
- **3 reverb sources**: Tape, BBD, dirty BBD
- **3 room modes per source**: Room, Hall, Cathedral
- **CV control** over size, feedback, tone, dry/wet, and preset
- **FREEZE** — manual button and CV gate input
- **Stereo spin** — adjustable width and rotation
- **10 preset patches** (save/recall)
- **Stereo I/O**, 48 kHz / 24-bit, −108 dB dynamic range
- **Power**: 120 mA (+12 V) / 39 mA (−12 V)
- Algorithms developed with **112dB**
