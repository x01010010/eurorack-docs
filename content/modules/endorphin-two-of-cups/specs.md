## Notes

- The mk1 **TWO OF CUPS** is discontinued. The current variant is the **Two of Cups M**, which adds MIDI (TRS Type A).
- Factory sound library by Julia Bondar is pre-loaded. Extended library available from the Endorphin.es sample library page (packs by Overmono, Nicolas Bougaieff, Maman Kusters, Nero Bellum).
- Firmware V3 (December 2025) is the final release for the mk1 hardware.
