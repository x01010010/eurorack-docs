# Overview

**TWO OF CUPS** is a 6 HP dual sample player from Endorphin.es. Two completely independent voices play WAV files directly from microSD with zero-latency, sample-accurate triggering — no buffering delay between trigger and audio. Each voice has access to 8 banks of up to 32 samples, giving 256 sounds per voice ready to switch under CV at any time.

The module packs pitch tracking (1 V/oct), a gate input, and a META CV input for scanning through sample banks into a minimal panel. A single PARAM knob per voice cycles through six parameter pages: envelope decay time, output volume, pitch offset, sample select, loop toggle, and reverse toggle. Despite its size, it handles drum one-shots, melodic hits, atmospheric textures, and looping playback.

The factory sound library by Julia Bondar is included on the microSD. Additional packs by Overmono, Nicolas Bougaieff, Maman Kusters, and Nero Bellum are available from the Endorphin.es sample library.

**TWO OF CUPS** (mk1) is discontinued. The successor, **Two of Cups M**, adds MIDI.
