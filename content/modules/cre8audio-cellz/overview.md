# Overview

**CELLZ** is a 12 HP programmable CV touch pad from Cre8audio. It presents 16 capacitive touch-sensitive pads in a 4×4 grid, each of which stores two independent CV values that can be programmed by the user. Touch a pad and both CVs appear simultaneously at the two outputs. It functions equally as a hands-on controller (play it like a keyboard, assigning pitches and velocities/timbres to individual pads) or as a sequencer (use the LFO trigger inputs to step automatically through the pads).

Each pad stores a 12-bit value on each of its two outputs — roughly 4,096 steps of resolution. The module operates in either **semi-tone quantized** mode (values snap to semitone intervals, useful for pitch sequences) or **free-scale** mode (full 0–10 V range without snapping, useful for CV and parameter control).

When multiple pads are touched simultaneously, **CELLZ** arpeggiates between them at a speed set by the SPEED knob. Two LFO trigger inputs accept signals from an external LFO (or the CHIPZ LFO outputs) to cycle automatically through the active pads — LFO Trigger 1 steps at one speed and LFO Trigger 2 at another, allowing two independent looping sequences from the same grid.

**CELLZ** is the companion to **CHIPZ** in Cre8audio's NiftyBUNDLE.

## At a Glance

- **12 HP**, 30 mm deep
- **16 touch-sensitive pads** (4×4 grid), each stores 2× CV values (12-bit)
- **Dual CV outputs**: 0–10 V (free scale) or ~2–4 V / ~4–6 V (quantized mode)
- **Quantized mode**: semi-tone intervals; **free-scale mode**: full range
- **Arpeggiate**: hold multiple pads — steps between them at SPEED setting
- **LFO Trigger 1 + 2 inputs**: auto-cycle pads from external LFO signals
- Minimum trigger voltage: 0.8 V
- **Power**: 3 mA (+12 V) / 2 mA (−12 V) / 27 mA (+5 V)
