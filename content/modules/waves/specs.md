| Input | Range |
| --- | --- |
| Slope CV | -8 V to +8 V |
| Frequency CV | -8 V to +8 V |
| Smoothness CV | -8 V to +8 V |
| Shape CV | -8 V to +8 V |
| Shift/Level CV | -8 V to +8 V |
| V/Oct | -3 V to +5 V |
| Trig | 5 Vpp |
| Clock | 5 Vpp |
