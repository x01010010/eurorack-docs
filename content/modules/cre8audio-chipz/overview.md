# Overview

**CHIPZ** is a 12 HP dual VCO and dual LFO module from Cre8audio, part of the NiftyBUNDLE alongside the Cellz touchpad sequencer. It was designed with lo-fi chiptune character in mind — the oscillators are modelled after the gritty, digital-adjacent sound of classic game console audio chips — but the module is a fully general-purpose dual oscillator with a wide range of waveforms.

**Chip1** has five waveforms (sine, triangle, saw, square, noise) with continuous morphing via a knob, plus an integrated filter that darkens the output and accepts CV control. **Chip2** also has five waveforms with morphing, plus integrated pulse width (duty cycle) control with CV. The two oscillators share V/Oct inputs for pitch control.

The dual LFO section provides two simultaneous outputs at different rates: LFO Out2 runs at 4× the speed of LFO Out1. This specific ratio is designed for use with Cellz — connecting both LFO outputs to Cellz's two LFO trigger inputs creates a cycling sequencer with two different loop rates. Both LFO outputs are unipolar (0–5 V) and share the same five waveforms.

**Important**: the Filter CV and Width CV inputs only accept positive voltages. Negative voltages into either input cause both oscillators and the LFOs to behave erratically.

## At a Glance

- **12 HP**, 30 mm deep
- **Chip1**: sine/tri/saw/sq/noise, integrated filter, filter CV input, V/Oct
- **Chip2**: sine/tri/saw/sq/noise, integrated pulse width, width CV input, V/Oct
- **Dual LFO**: same five waveforms, LFO Out1 (75 mHz–2 Hz), LFO Out2 (370 mHz–10 Hz, 4× LFO1 speed)
- LFO outputs unipolar 0–5 V (LFO Out2: 0–5.8 V)
- **Power**: 35 mA (+12 V) / 19 mA (−12 V) / 30 mA (+5 V)
