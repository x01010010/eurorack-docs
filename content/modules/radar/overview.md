# Overview

Radar is a contact microphone interface and envelope/gate follower. It converts physical vibrations detected by a contact mic or small acoustic sensor into audio, envelope CV, and gate signals usable in a Eurorack patch. A GAIN knob and attack/decay/sensitivity switches make it adaptable to quiet acoustic sources (guitar body, table surface) and louder percussion (drum shell, floor).

Three outputs — audio, envelope (0–10V), and gate (0–5V) — allow Radar to simultaneously drive signal processing, modulation, and rhythm logic from a single physical sound source.
