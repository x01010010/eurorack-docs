# Overview

Halos is a modal resonator based on the Mutable Instruments Rings architecture. It simulates the resonant behaviour of physical objects — strings, plates, bowls, tubes — by applying modal synthesis to an audio or trigger input. The result ranges from realistic acoustic instrument timbres to abstract metallic or crystalline resonances.

Halos can operate as a mono resonator, a stereo resonator, or a polyphonic resonator with up to 4 simultaneous voices. All five main parameters (frequency, structure, brightness, damping, position) are voltage-controllable. The three outputs (OUT, ODD, EVEN) provide the full resonator signal plus odd and even harmonic partials as separate signals.
