# Overview

The 150 is a multi-function utility module based on the Roland System 100M 150 Ring Mod/Noise/S&H/LFO. It combines four distinct processors in a single panel: a ring modulator, two noise generators (white and pink, each with two outputs), a sample-and-hold with external or internal clock, and a multi-waveform LFO.

Each section is independently usable. Together they cover a wide range of modulation and sound design needs — from metallic ring-mod timbres to randomised CV, rhythmic noise bursts, and slow modulation.
