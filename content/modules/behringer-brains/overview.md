# Overview

Brains is a 24-engine macro-oscillator based on the Mutable Instruments Plaits architecture. A single module covers analogue oscillator models, physical modelling, wavetable, and noise synthesis — all navigated via a bank/model matrix and a small OLED display. Every parameter has a direct knob and a CV-over-attenuverter input, making complex timbres fully patchable.

Two simultaneous outputs (OUT1 and OUT2) provide the main sound and an auxiliary signal. The auxiliary output changes character per engine: in additive and spectral modes it carries a complementary component; in physical models it often carries an internal resonator or body signal.
