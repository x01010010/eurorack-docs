# Tips

- **Fill dead HP**: At 2 HP the **HPO** is ideal for filling the last gap in a full skiff without sacrificing a useful slot to a large output module.
- **Mono normalisation**: If you only have a mono signal, patch into L only — it normalises to both channels so you hear it in both ears without an extra mult.
- **Level trim**: The VOLUME knob doubles as a rough attenuator. If your mix is very loud, turn it down before connecting headphones to avoid hearing damage.
- **Use with a mixer**: Pair the **HPO** with ALM's Tangle Quartet, MEGA-TANG, or any stereo mixer for a full mixing-and-monitoring chain in a compact skiff.
- **Skiff depth**: At 38 mm, the **HPO** fits virtually every Eurorack skiff on the market — no need to measure first.
