# Panel

## Controls

| Control | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| VOLUME | Single knob controlling headphone output level |

## Inputs

| Jack | Signal | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| L (left) | Audio in — left channel | Normalised to right when R unplugged |
| R (right) | Audio in — right channel | |

## Outputs

| Jack | Signal |
|------|--------|
| Headphone (3.5 mm TRS) | Stereo headphone output |
