# Overview

**DYSTOPIA** is a 10 HP noise processor from Dreadbox combining a multi-type noise generator, a bit crusher, a random gate generator, and a dual 12 dB filter. Together these sections cover a wide range of sound design territory — from subtle texture and color to full destruction.

The noise generator offers six color modes (white, pink, blue, scatter, digital, and pitched), each with a distinct frequency character. Routing it through the bit crusher reduces the sample rate from 8 kHz down to 1 Hz, introducing lo-fi artifacts and rhythmic grunge. The digital noise mode doubles as a random gate generator for clocking or triggering other modules. Two parallel filters — a 12 dB low-pass and a 12 dB high-pass — sculpt the final output. Patching an external signal replaces the internal noise source, making **DYSTOPIA** a crusher-filter for any audio or CV input.

LED indicators on each section show the current CV sum, making modulation levels visible without metering.

**DYSTOPIA** is part of the Dreadbox Chromatic Modules series. Version 1.0 is discontinued.

## At a Glance

- **10 HP**, 35 mm deep
- **6 noise types**: white, pink (white→pink), blue (white→blue), scatter, digital, pitched
- **Bit crusher**: 1 Hz – 8 kHz sample rate
- **Random gate generator** from digital noise
- **Dual filter**: parallel 12 dB LP + 12 dB HP
- **External input**: replaces internal noise for processing outside signals
- **LED indicators** for CV signal levels per section
- **Power**: 56 mA (+12 V) / 67 mA (−12 V)
