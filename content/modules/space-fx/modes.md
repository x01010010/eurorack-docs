# Modes

Space FX provides 32 algorithm slots selectable via the PROGRAM knob. The exact program list varies by firmware version but covers the following categories:

## Reverb Algorithms

Hall, room, plate, spring, and shimmer reverb types. PARAM 1 typically controls reverb time; PARAM 2 controls high-frequency damping or pre-delay. Hall and plate algorithms are the most neutral and widely applicable.

## Delay Algorithms

Mono and stereo delay, ping-pong delay, tape delay emulation. PARAM 1 controls delay time; PARAM 2 controls feedback. TAP button sets delay time by rhythm — press in time with the music. Ping-pong alternates echoes between left and right channels.

## Modulation Algorithms

Chorus, flanger, phaser, and ensemble. These primarily affect the stereo spread and movement of a signal rather than adding reverberation or echo. PARAM 1 typically controls rate; PARAM 2 controls depth.

## Combination Algorithms

Several programs combine reverb + chorus, delay + reverb, or other pairings. These reduce the need for multiple modules in a basic effects chain.

## Parameter Access

PARAM 1 and PARAM 2 control the most important parameters for the selected algorithm. On some algorithms, holding or pressing TAP accesses a third parameter (PARAM 3) via the button — the display confirms which parameter is active.
