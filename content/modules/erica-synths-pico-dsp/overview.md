# Overview

**PICO DSP** is a 3 HP stereo effects processor from Erica Synths, part of the Pico Series. It packs eight custom effects into one of the smallest footprints in the format, making it a go-to choice for adding time and space to a patch without sacrificing rack real estate.

The module is the compact sibling of the Black Hole DSP. Each of its eight effects has two adjustable parameters specific to that effect (e.g., delay time and feedback, reverb size and decay). The first parameter of every effect is CV-controllable, allowing real-time modulation. A dry/wet knob blends the processed signal against the dry input, and the output is stereo even though the input is mono.

Effects are selected via a rotary switch with colour-coded positions, making the current effect immediately visible from across a rack.

## At a Glance

- **3 HP**, 35 mm deep
- **8 custom stereo effects** — delay, pitch shift, granular, reverb, saturation, Leslie, overdrive/bitcrush
- **2 parameters per effect**, independently adjustable
- **CV over Parameter 1** — full sweep with ±5 V
- **Dry/Wet control**
- **Stereo output**, mono input
- **Colour-coded effect selector**
- **Power**: 82 mA (+12 V) / 20 mA (−12 V)
