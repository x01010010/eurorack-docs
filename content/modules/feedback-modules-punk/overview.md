# Overview

**PUNK** is a compact 6 HP distortion processor from Feedback Modules that crams two distinct distortion flavors into minimal space: a fat, saturated preamp inspired by 1980s cassette recorder mixer circuits, and a classic guitar pedal fuzz with switchable diode or MOSFET character.

Both distortion types have independent gain controls, allowing them to be used separately or blended together to taste. After distortion, the signal passes through a Baxandall two-band equalizer—the same shelving EQ used in professional audio equipment and home stereo amplifiers—for transparent, musical tone shaping. A final input level control and a blend knob let you balance clean and distorted signals with surgical precision.

In just 6 HP, **PUNK** delivers serious attitude: from subtle grit and harmonic fattening to aggressive, heavily mangled overtones. The dual-distortion architecture and EQ make it surprisingly versatile for both drums, bass, leads, and ambient textures.

## At a Glance

- **6 HP** dual-flavor distortion processor
- **FET preamp** — fat, vintage cassette-mixer vibe
- **Guitar fuzz** — diode or MOSFET switchable
- **Individual gain controls** — use each type separately or blended
- **Baxandall two-band EQ** — transparent bass and treble shaping
- **Input level** + **blend control** — dial in the exact character
- **Low power draw** — +12 V @ 45 mA, −12 V @ 25 mA
