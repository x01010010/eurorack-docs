# Modes

Brains organises its 24 engines into banks. Press BANK to cycle banks and MODEL to cycle models within the selected bank. The OLED display names both.

## Bank A — Classic Analogue-style

Models include virtual analogue (sawtooth stacks, pulse-width), two-operator FM, and formant/speech approximation. Familiar pitch-tracking oscillator territory — TRIG resets the phase.

## Bank B — Waveshaping & Additive

Chord generator (OUT2 carries individual notes), harmonic oscillator (HARMONICS sets partial spread), and wavefolder variants. Dense harmonic content from subtle to extreme.

## Bank C — Physical Models

String model (Karplus-Strong variant), modal resonator, and bowed string. TRIG excites the resonator; MORPH adjusts decay and body character. OUT2 often carries the resonator signal independently.

## Bank D — Noise & Spectral

Granular cloud, particle noise, and spectral comb filter. These engines produce evolving textures rather than pitched tones. V/OCT still tracks pitch where relevant.

## Notes

- Holding BANK while pressing MODEL performs a long-press action (calibration / factory reset depending on firmware).
- The MODEL CV input sweeps through models continuously — useful for sequencing engine changes.
- All engines share the same four-parameter interface; parameter meanings differ per engine as labelled in the display.
