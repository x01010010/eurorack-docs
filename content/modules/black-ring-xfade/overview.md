# Overview

**BLACK RING-XFADE** is a 10 HP amplitude-modulation module from Erica Synths. It combines four building blocks in a single panel: two ring modulators/waveshapers, a voltage-controlled crossfader, and a VCA.

The ring modulator design is unconventional. A standard ring modulator produces output only when both a signal and a carrier are present, outputting the sum and difference frequencies of the two. The **BLACK RING-XFADE** differs: with no carrier present, the signal passes through unaltered. As carrier level increases from zero, the circuit transitions smoothly from clean passthrough through waveshaping into full ring modulation. This makes it far more musical to dial in — there is a continuous spectrum of effects between transparent and fully ring-modulated.

The crossfader connects the two ring modulator outputs (or external sources) and has both manual control and a CV input, with selectable AC (audio) or DC (CV) coupling. The VCA rounds out the module with a selectable bias, allowing it to function as a standard VCA (zero output at zero CV) or with an initial offset for amplitude modulation applications.

## At a Glance

- **10 HP**, 35 mm deep
- **2× ring modulators/waveshapers** — unconventional design, waveshapes with partial carrier
- **Carrier level adjustment** per ring modulator
- **Voltage-controlled crossfader** — manual + CV, selectable AC/DC coupling
- **VCA** with selectable bias
- **Audio level**: 10 Vptp
- **CV level (full span)**: ±5 V
- **Power**: 38 mA (+12 V) / 32 mA (−12 V)
