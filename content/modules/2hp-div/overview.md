# Overview

**DIV** is a 2 HP dual-channel voltage-controlled clock divider and multiplier from 2hp. A single incoming clock signal can be split into two independent rhythmic streams, each running at a different division or multiplication of the source tempo.

Each channel has its own RATE knob and CV input, selecting from division/multiplication values of 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, or 16. Patching CV allows the rate to change in real time under sequencer or LFO control, creating dynamic polyrhythms and evolving rhythmic patterns that would require multiple dedicated modules to achieve otherwise.

**DIV** can also be pushed into audio-rate territory with fast enough clock inputs, producing chiptune-style tones and digital artifacts.
