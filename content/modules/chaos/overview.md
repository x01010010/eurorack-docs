# Overview

Chaos is a random sampler and gate generator based on the Mutable Instruments Marbles architecture. It produces three gate/trigger outputs (T1, T2, T3) and four voltage outputs (X1, X2, X3, Y) with continuously adjustable randomness, bias, and distribution.

Two main sections — the T section (time/gates) and the X section (voltages) — operate independently but can be linked. The T section controls the timing and probability of triggers; the X section controls the voltage distribution, range, and quantisation of the random CV outputs. Together they allow generative patches ranging from mildly randomised sequences to fully chaotic textures.
